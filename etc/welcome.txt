StoRM Backend Server


This is the backend part of StoRM, a Storage Resource Manager 
 (SRM) v2.2 implementation, resulting from a 
collaboration between the Istituto Nazionale di Fisica Nucleare INFN-CNAF centre 
 and the Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical 
Physics ICTP - EGRID Project
